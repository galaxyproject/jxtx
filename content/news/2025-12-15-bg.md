# JXTX + CSHL 2026 Biology of Genomes Scholarship

<Image alt="JXTX + CSHL 2026 Biology of Genomes Scholarship" image={props.images[0]}></Image>

<figcaption>JXTX + CSHL 2026 Biology of Genomes Scholarship</figcaption>


The JXTX Foundation and Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory announce the 2026 Biology of Genomes Scholarship. JXTX and CSHL will provide support to three outstanding graduate students in genomics and data sciences to attend the [2026 CSHL Biology of Genomes Conference](https://meetings.cshl.edu/meetings.aspx?meet=GENOME).

## The scholarship will be awarded in two tiers:

- The first tier will be awarded to one individual and will cover conference registration and transportation to and from the conference
- The second tier will be awarded to two individuals and will cover conference registration only


## Key dates:

- Registration site opens: Monday, December 16, 2025
- Deadline for applications: Friday, February 13, 2026
- Winners announced: Tuesday, February 24, 2026

Applicants should be planning to present their work at the conference, either as a talk or as a poster.
