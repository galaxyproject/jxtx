# JXTX + JJ Fund GCC 2026 Scholarships

<Image alt="JXTX + JJ Fund GCC 2026 Scholarships" image={props.images[0]}></Image>

<figcaption>JXTX + JJ Fund GCC 2026 Scholarships: Supporting travel to the Galaxy Community Conference</figcaption>

The JXTX Foundation and the [JJ Fund](/scholarships/jj-fund) announce the 2026 GCC Scholarship program. This program will support outstanding graduate students and postdoctoral researchers to attend the [Galaxy Community Conference 2026](https://galaxyproject.org/events/gcc2026/) in Clermont-Ferrand, France.

## JXTX Scholarships

JXTX scholarships will be awarded in two tiers:

- The first tier will be awarded to two individuals and will cover conference registration plus transportation to and from the conference (up to US$1,000).
- The second tier will be awarded to two individuals and will cover conference registration only.

## JJ Fund Travel Fellowships

- Two fellowships will be awarded to graduate students or postdoctoral researchers to attend GCC 2026.
- Each fellowship will cover the cost of travel, up to $600 per recipient.

- Application deadline: TBD

## Application Process

Applicants will need to submit:

- **One-page write-up** describing: a) their scientific interests, b) their interest in the Galaxy platform, and c) why they are interested in attending GCC 2026. Applicants should have submitted an abstract to the conference and include the title in this write-up.
- **Curriculum vitae**
- **Reference letter** from advisor

Email all documents to: [jj-fund@jxtxfoundation.org](mailto:jj-fund@jxtxfoundation.org). Any submission missing required documents will not be considered.

## Review Criteria

Applications will be evaluated based on:

- **Quality of application** - should articulate a solid reasoning for why they want to attend a Galaxy conference and learn more about the platform and community
- **Early career stage** - students and post-docs are preferred over staff and faculty
- **New to Galaxy** - applicants who are new to the Galaxy community and eager to learn more are favored
- **Diversity** - applicants from underrepresented groups and applicants from the University of Minnesota or other upper Midwestern schools are particularly encouraged to apply

Anyone in the early stages of their career can apply and will be considered. Awards will go to applicants with the overall strongest applications.
