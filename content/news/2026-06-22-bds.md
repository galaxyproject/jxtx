# JXTX + CSHL 2026 Biological Data Science Scholarship

<Image alt="JXTX + CSHL 2026 Biological Data Science Scholarship" image={props.images[0]}></Image>

<figcaption>JXTX + CSHL 2026 Biological Data Science Scholarship</figcaption>


The JXTX Foundation and Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory announce the 2026 Biological Data Science Scholarship. JXTX and CSHL will provide support to three outstanding graduate students in genomics and data sciences to attend the [2026 CSHL Biological Data Science Conference](https://meetings.cshl.edu/meetings.aspx?meet=DATA).

## The scholarship will be awarded in two tiers:

- The first tier will be awarded to one individual and will cover conference registration and transportation to and from the conference
- The second tier will be awarded to two individuals and will cover conference registration only


## Key dates:

- Registration site opens: Monday, June 22, 2026
- Deadline for applications: Friday, August 7, 2026
- Winners announced: Wednesday, August 12, 2026

Applicants should be planning to present their work at the conference, either as a talk or as a poster.
